
El proyecto está destinado a un perfil de usuario que valora la claridad visual y la presentación directa de información personal y profesional.

Rango etario: Predominantemente entre 15 y 35 años, abarcando desde compañeros de estudio hasta posibles reclutadores o docentes.

Nacionalidad: Argentina.

Ubicación geográfica: Contexto urbano, orientado principalmente a la región de Resistencia, Chaco, debido a la vinculación con la institución educativa EET N° 24.

Variables adicionales relevantes:

Nivel educativo: Estudiantes de nivel secundario técnico o profesionales del sector tecnológico y gastronómico.

Ocupación: Estudiantes, desarrolladores junior y entusiastas del diseño web.

Alfabetización digital: Media-alta; usuarios familiarizados con interfaces de redes sociales modernas.

Dispositivos de acceso: Acceso habitual mediante computadoras de escritorio y dispositivos móviles (diseño adaptativo).

Idioma principal: Español.


Design Inspirations
Facebook's Profile Architecture: I used the structural logic of a social network profile to organize information into clear, recognizable blocks, ensuring immediate user familiarity.

Modern Dark Mode Systems: The color palette (dark grays and blacks) is inspired by professional IDEs and modern portfolio sites to reduce eye strain and provide a sleek, "tech-focused" look.

Minimalist Card Design: The use of cards with subtle borders and shadows was inspired by Google's Material Design to create a clear hierarchy of information.

Creative Process Documentation
The creative process for this personal landing page evolved through several key stages:

Conceptualization: The initial idea was to create a profile inspired by Facebook but stripped of all interactive or "editable" elements. The goal was to transform a social media layout into a static, harmonious presentation site.

Structural Decisions: I moved away from a standard wide layout to a centralized vertical flow. This decision was made to ensure the site looks natural on both desktop and mobile devices, focusing the reader's attention on a single column of content.

Visual Refinement:

Discarded Alternatives: I initially considered a light theme (Facebook's classic blue/white), but discarded it in favor of a Dark Gray Mode to achieve a more "natural" and less "AI-generated" appearance.

Iconography: I integrated FontAwesome icons to provide visual cues for each section (Hobbies, Education, Interests), making the text-heavy sections easier to scan.

Personalization of the Frame: A specific design choice was made regarding the profile picture. Instead of a standard circle, we implemented a square frame with a thin black border, separated by internal padding. This adds a unique, custom-made feel that differentiates it from generic templates.
